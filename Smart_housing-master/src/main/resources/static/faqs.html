Frequently Asked Questions

How does HostelHub help me find a hostel?

HostelHub connects students with verified off-campus hostels. You can filter results by price, distance, amenities, and room type to find your perfect match.

Is it free to use HostelHub?

Yes! Searching and viewing listings is completely free. Some property owners may charge a small booking or reservation fee when confirming your stay.

Are the hostels verified?

We verify listings through documents and reviews from students. Verified listings are marked with a green “✔ Verified” badge for trust and safety.

Can I contact the landlord directly?

Yes. Once you find a hostel you’re interested in, you can contact the landlord directly through our secure chat or call options provided on the listing.

How do I list my property on HostelHub?

Simply create a landlord account, add your property details, photos, and pricing, and publish it. Our team reviews listings to ensure quality and accuracy.
